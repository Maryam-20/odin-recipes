PepperSoup Recipe

Peppersoup is one of the most delicious Nigeria foods. I want to talk about Assorted meat recipe. I have come to learn, in the last few years that several recipes exist. This one, however, is made with assorted meat. You will learn about the ingredient/spices used for pepper soup also.

INGREDIENT

  • 1kg of assorted meat(goat or cow)
  • Seeds of Ehu
  • Scent Leaves
  • Stock Cubes
  • Tablespoons of ground crayfish
  • 1 cup of sliced onions

STEPS

  1. Cook the meat with some simple ingredient for 10 minute
  2. Add 3 cups of water and cook for 30-50 minutes until the meat becomes tender
  3. Add peppersoup spice, the ground ehu, uziza, crayfish and remainin g sonions
  4. Add the Paprika
  5. Add the sliced utazi, salt and pepper soup to taste
